About Us

Our Mission

A & B Fabricators has been an industry leading stair guardrail, handrail and decorative metals company, for over 60 years.

A key element in the success of A & B Fabricators has been our in-house teams of industry leaders. Their proficiency in design, engineering, project management, and installation make A & B Fabricators the preferred sub-contractor. We take great pride in building and maintaining lasting partnerships with our clients.
